EMSE 6035: Marketing Analytics for Design Decisions


Description:

This is the course website for the EMSE graduate course “Marketing Analytics for Design Decisions” at the George Washington University. The course teaching analytical tools to inform design decisions in an uncertain, competitive market; topics include consumer choice modeling, programming in the R programming language, survey design, conjoint analysis, optimization, market simulation, and professional communication skills.
